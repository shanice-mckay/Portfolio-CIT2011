Academic Life and Me

I believe that my curiosity drives my interest in self-improvement. It makes me want to explore new experiences.

Gratitude helps me appreciate the opportunities I have, even when the workload feels overwhelming.

Public relations takes up a lot of my time, but this has occasionally impacted my academic life negatively. Balancing the demands of it with my schoolwork has been challenging especially this semester. However, I want to better understand my tendencies so I can know how to manage both responsibilities without sacrificing my progress in either of them.

My Hero: Tyler, the Creator

Quote: “Bro, just be proud of whatever you're making.” — Tyler, the Creator

Tyler, the Creator is my hero because of his unapologetic individuality and creative genius. He openly explores different forms of art, from music to fashion to filmmaking, which inspires me as a multipotentialite. His journey from being an unconventional rapper all those years ago to a Grammy-winning artist now shows that staying authentic can lead to success. His has this insane ability of creating worlds through his work which encourages me. One thing about him is that he always believed in himself even when the people around him didn't understand the vision.
